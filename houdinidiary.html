Vellum Cloth & Fluid Pie Birth

Inspired by the work of extraweg while trying to figure out their technique.

Vellum cloth with rest lengths driven by an attribute ramp. Longer rest lengths around the hole at the centre allow the cloth to scrunch up. A POP attract node pulls the hole closed.

The pie model from Megascans and vellum fluid are stored in a pinned angled box behind the hole. A POP force gradually pushes the objects through the cloth. Low particle separation and filtering were used to stabilise the fluid simulation.

Rendered in Solaris with Karma XPU and MaterialX (AMD Library).

RBD Shroomies

Megascans mushrooms being thrown around in a RBD simulation with POP attraction. The camera follows the movement and resets to create a looping animation.

Vellum Hair

Meshed vellum hair using lines and the sweep SOP. Face stroking was achieved using POP attract to direct the hair toward the head.

Vellum Balloon

Vellum balloon spheres within an animated spherical collision mesh. Some POP wind to make things move a little.
